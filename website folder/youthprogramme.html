Saviour of Humanity

Moorish Leader['s] Historical Message to America

Humanity; The Children of Humanity Must be Lifted from the unwholesome depth[s] of poverty; misery and suffering and Place on The Solid Rock of Salvation

In this electrified age; Our Youth are racing into this life without complete knowledge of where They are going; and/or what the end will be; when riches seem to be their onlly pursuit; to be obtained anyway and/or at any cost; when selishness; avarice; greed and/or lust dominate their very being; Humanity in general is left at the mercy of those who have no mercy in them

It is Truly Wonderful and Astounding to see one come into this mad human drama for The Sole Purpose of Saving Humanity

Our WombMen; Men and Children should be taught to believe in the capacity of Our Group to Succeed in business; in spite of the trial[s] and failure[s] of some of them

Trial[s] and failure[s] in business are by no mean[s] confirned to any particular group of people

We have many Men and WombMen Among Our People who are qalified; bothe by training and experience; who are Shining Light[s] in the business world of all the people

In ConClusion:

I urge you to remember there is work enough for all to do in Helping to Build a better World

the problem[s] of life are largely social and economic

In A ProFound Sense; they are moral and Spiritual

Have A Lofty Conception[s] of Your Duties to Your Country and Fellow Man in general and especially those with whom You deal

This include[s] such as: Honesty and Righteousness as will cause You to put YourSelf in the other fellow['s] place

Look for The Best in other[s] and give them The Best that is in You

Have A Deepe Appreciation for WombManHood

Brighten The Hope[s] of Our Youth in order that Their Courage be Increased to Dare and Do Wondrous Thing[s]

Adhere at all time[s] to The Principle[s] of:

Love;
Truth;
Peace;
Freedom
and Justice

Prophet Noble Drew Ali
